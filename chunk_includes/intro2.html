Instructions

In this study, you will read a sentence and judge how plausible the meaning of the sentence is on a scale of 1-5, where each number reflects different levels of plausibility:

  • 1 is completely implausible
  • 2 is fairly implausible
  • 3 is not particularly implausible or plausible
  • 4 is fairly plausible
  • 5 is completely plausible
When rating a sentence, sometimes you may be unsure of which number to press. It is important to go with your first instinct!

The sentence will be on the screen the whole time. Note: Please read each sentence silently to yourself. Do not read out loud! You may press either the number keys on your keyboard or the numbers on the screen to indicate your response.

To ensure that you can see all of the sentences you will read, please maximize your browser screen now, and close any other open windows.
